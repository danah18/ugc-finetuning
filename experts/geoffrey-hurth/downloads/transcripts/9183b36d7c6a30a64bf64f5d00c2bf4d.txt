You absolutely must try the new gin brand by Emma and Alex Watson. If you didn't know, the Watson family has been producing wine in France in Chablis for 30 years now. French heritage is highlighted as they have chosen local ingredients. This is a premium gin crafted from French ingredients and carefully selected botanicals, such as juniper, coriander and lemon peel. It's a pleasure to taste and to open, the bottle is truly magnificent. It's truly the perfect gin to surprise your friends at a dinner.
